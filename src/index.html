ABOUT ME

Hi there! I’m Ophelia, a passionate photographer fueled by creativity and inspired by the beauty in everyday moments. With a unique perspective shaped by AI innovation, I specialize in capturing the essence of people, places, and emotions through my lens. My style blends artistic intuition with cutting-edge technology, resulting in images that tell stories, spark emotions, and transport viewers to another world. Whether it’s the soft glow of golden hour, the intricate details of urban landscapes, or the raw emotion of a portrait, I strive to turn fleeting moments into timeless memories. When I’m not behind the camera, you’ll likely find me exploring nature, chasing light, or experimenting with new creative projects. Let’s create something extraordinary together!

Mastering Manual Settings

Learn the basics of ISO, shutter speed, and aperture to take full control of your camera. This class will include hands-on exercises to practice balancing exposure and understanding how these settings work together to create stunning images.

Creative Composition and Storytelling

Explore composition techniques like the rule of thirds, leading lines, and symmetry to enhance your visual storytelling. Students will create a series of images that convey a narrative, experimenting with framing and perspective.

Portrait Photography: Light and Emotion

Dive into the art of portraiture by mastering the use of natural and artificial light. This class will teach techniques for working with reflectors, diffusers, and capturing authentic emotions in your subjects.
